Teleportation

Teleportation is possible?

To be clear, I'm talking about instantaneous teleportation, it's means at the speed of light. Then in a way, yes and no.

Why no? You should already know that when your speed increases, time slows down, this phenomenon is called special relativity, said Albert Einstein, but once you reach the speed of light (299,792,458 km/s), time becomes infinitely slow. Which is logically impossible.
A second thing you should know that when you go faster and faster your mass increases, and as for the first example when you reach this speed 299,792,458 km/s your mass becomes infinitely large. There are other examples but I think you get the concept, going at the speed of light breaks the rules of the universe.

Info: How do photons of light reach the speed of light? The photons of lights not of mass they are not affected by the law of the special relativity.

But just before I said yes teleportation is possible! How?
Well, as I said just before, it's strictly impossible to exceed the speed of light, but only inside spacetime. How it inside space-time? Let's designate spacetime with a sheet paper and your spaceship with a pen, if somehow you manage to warp spacetime and move your pen that designates your ship faster than light, your ship will go from point A to point B faster than light and if we look inside spacetime, this pen would not have moved. You may be wondering, but how do you reshape space-time?
Well, the most visible phenomenon for reshaping space-time is gravity. Even you who have mass manage to modify the space-time but it is much less visible because you have much less mass than a planet.

In conclusion teleportation is technically possible but we are not there yet.
